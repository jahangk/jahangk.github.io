CS2 portfolio optimisation

GitHub →

Applied Modern Portfolio Theory to investment-grade CS2 cosmetic items (discontinued cases and sticker capsules). Built ARIMAX price forecasts, estimated the covariance matrix with Ledoit-Wolf shrinkage, and solved a long-only quadratic optimisation problem. Evaluated strategy with a walk-forward backtest in R.

Gender & educational attainment in Tanzania

Report →

Empirical analysis of the gender gap in years of schooling using World Bank NPS Wave 5 cross-sectional data. Estimated OLS and IV/2SLS models in R, identified a sign reversal in the female coefficient across age subsamples, and assessed structural limitations of cross-sectional data for decomposing cumulative outcome variables.

An analysis of Australia's NHA

Read PDF →

Evaluates why Australia's National Housing Accord is falling short of its 1.2-million-home target, tracing the shortfall to zoning restrictions, construction labour shortages, and a housing mix skewed toward detached homes. Benchmarks the Accord against New Zealand, Japan, and Singapore's housing policies, and proposes reforms spanning density targets, negative gearing, and demand-side incentives for first-home buyers.
